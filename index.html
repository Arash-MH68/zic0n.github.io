About Zic0n

Zic0n is an applied civil engineering practice co-founded by two Ph.D. P.E.s—one rooted in geotechnical/geo-structural design and instrumentation, the other in transportation/roadway design and asphalt materials. Together, we provide end-to-end support: site investigations, roadway and drainage plans, foundation and wall systems, pavement design and rehab, lab testing, and analytics.

  • Foundations (shallow/deep, drilled shafts, piles), slope stability, seepage, ground improvement, dams/levees (select services).
  • Roadway geometrics, alignments, guardrail (MGS), drainage inlets/culverts, signing & striping, quantities & cost estimates (DOT/Caltrans).
  • Pavements (AASHTO & M-E), asphalt binder/mixture testing, QA/QC, forensic & rehab strategies, life-cycle assessment (LCA).
  • Instrumentation programs (thermal/adfreeze, settlement), data QA/QC, parametric & Monte Carlo analyses, reporting pipelines.
