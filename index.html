About Zic0n

Zic0n is an applied engineering practice focused on geotechnical, pavement, and materials engineering—complemented by modern analytics and automation. We support owners and design teams from early planning through construction and performance verification.

  • Foundation design: shallow/deep, drilled shafts, pile embedment, and uplift/adfreeze.
  • Slope stability, seepage, and ground improvement options analyses.
  • Pavement systems: design, rehabilitation strategies, and life-cycle optimization.
  • Instrumentation & lab programs: thermal conductivity, adfreeze, and performance testing.
